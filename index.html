Hi! I am a Research Assistant at Florida International University (FIU). My research focuses on Agentic AI, multimodal explainability, post-training methods for LLMs, and privacy/security in tool-augmented AI systems.

I received my B.Tech (Honors) in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Kottayam.

I am originally from Kerala, India, and outside of research I enjoy sports and outdoor activities.

I am always happy to connect for research collaboration and academic discussions.
